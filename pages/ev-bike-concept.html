Concept design

EV Bike Concept

A concept visual built around presentation, form, and a futuristic mobility feel.

Overview

What it is

  • A creative concept centered on an EV bike idea.
  • Built to show form, mood, and presentation.
  • Focused more on visual storytelling than engineering.

Problem

What I wanted to explore

  • How to present a concept with strong visual appeal.
  • How to make a futuristic product feel believable.
  • How to communicate mood through design alone.

Approach / Process

How I approached it

  • Started with the visual direction first.
  • Focused on silhouette, framing, and final look.
  • Refined the concept around presentation quality.

Design / Development

Main focus

  • Strong product mood and visual framing.
  • Clean concept presentation.
  • Polished look over heavy technical explanation.

Result / Outcome

What it shows

  • Ability to turn an idea into a polished concept.
  • Confidence in creative presentation work.
  • Good visual instinct around product storytelling.

Learnings

Takeaways

  • Concept work is strongest when the mood is clear.
  • Presentation can carry a project effectively.
  • Simple visuals can still feel premium and intentional.
