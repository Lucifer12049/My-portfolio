Gaming Console UI Designs

A concept UI series built to feel immersive, bold, and entertainment-first.

Overview

What it is

  • A gaming console interface concept.
  • Focused on multiple connected screens.
  • Designed to feel cinematic and modern.

Problem

What I wanted to solve

  • Gaming UIs can feel cluttered or generic.
  • I wanted stronger hierarchy and mood.
  • The experience needed to look exciting but stay usable.

Approach / Process

How I approached it

  • Started with visual direction and screen flow.
  • Built each screen as part of one connected system.
  • Focused on clarity, contrast, and atmosphere.

Design / Development

Visuals first

  • High-contrast visual hierarchy.
  • Entertainment-focused layouts.
  • Consistent feel across multiple screens.

Result / Outcome

What it shows

  • Comfort with strong UI presentation.
  • Ability to design a full visual system.
  • Clearer confidence in concept-driven interface work.

Learnings

What I learned

  • Visual energy still needs structure.
  • Consistency matters across every screen.
  • Presentation quality can elevate a concept quickly.
